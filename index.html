August, 2023 - Trip to Hawaii

This was my first time going to Hawaii. I was very excited to go and had a wonderful time with my family. Over the week that we were there, we went on many hikes, explored the many beaches Oahu has to offer, and most importantly, spent time with our extended family. The ocean was breathtaking, with colors ranging from turquoise to a dark blue, something I had never seen before. I also loved to just sit and watch the waves roll by; I had never experienced such large waves before. The sand was just like clay and together, everything felt unreal.
